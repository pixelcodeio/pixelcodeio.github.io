About

Our Story

We come from the three different aspects of mobile app development: product design, iOS dev, backend dev. We saw that there were many inefficiencies with design hand-off tools. It didn't make sense for designs to be done twice so we set out to build a tool to save teams time.

Our Mission

We want to make iOS app development as seamless as possible. No time wasted on silly pixel-coding. More time to focus on more important things. We want to help teams be more effective.
